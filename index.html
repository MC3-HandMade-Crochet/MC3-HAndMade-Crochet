About Me

I'm a Crocheter

Welcome to MC3 Handmade Crochet. This business is independently owned and operated by a single creator. I design and produce a wide range of handmade products, including crochet items, artwork, and videos, and also manage all social media platforms. You can follow MC3 Handmade Crochet on YouTube, Facebook, TikTok, and Instagram for updates and new releases. For inquiries, please contact me at mc3crochet4life@gmail.com. Due to the individualized nature of my work, advance notice is required for custom orders, especially if you need an item by a specific date. Production times vary: plushies generally require up to two weeks, while clothing items can take up to four weeks. Please note that all sales are final— refunds are not available.
